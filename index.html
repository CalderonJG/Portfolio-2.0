About

What began as mere curiousity turned into a craft...

  • 2017-2018

    How it started

    While working a contract job in New Mexico, I was preparing for the next step in my professional career. I began teaching myself SQL when I became interested in web development.

  • 2019

    "Down the Rabbit Hole..."

    While working, I unknowingly opened the Dev Tools in my browser! Curious on what I did, I spoke to some co-workers and asked what I did and how I could repeat the action. Down the rabbit hole I go...

  • 2020

    Going All In

    A global pandemic turned lives upside down. Loved ones are lost, self-quarantining, job loss...the pandemic has impacted everyone, including me. This is when I bet on myself to make a professional change after working in supply chain for over a decade.

  • 2021

    Blazing my own trail

    After completing my full-stack web development program at Ohio State, I immediately got to work: learning new languages, improving my previous applications, applying what I have learned to every new project. This has allowed me the freedom to truly multitask and enjoy helping others solve their problems!
